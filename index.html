Guided quad and buggy rides

Adventure on Offroad Roads!

Step into the world of adventure and discover the rugged nature like never before! Our guided quad and buggy tours take you along the most beautiful and challenging offroad routes. Whether you are looking for excitement, nature or just a unique experience, our rides offer something for everyone.

Offroad Experience:

Ride over dirt roads and challenging terrain. Our guides will ensure that you enter the adventure safely and in a controlled manner.

Guided Rides:

Our experienced guides will lead you through the most beautiful and often undiscovered paths. They will give instructions, tips and ensure that you get the most out of your ride.

Suitable for Everyone:

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rider, our tours are tailor-made for all levels. The quads and buggies are easy to operate and offer a comfortable ride, even on the roughest trails.

Groups or Individual:

Come alone, with friends or as a group! Our rides can be adapted to the number of participants, and we ensure a personal and unforgettable experience.

Safety and Comfort:

Safety is our top priority. You will receive extensive instructions in advance on how to drive the vehicles and how to drive on off-road terrain.
