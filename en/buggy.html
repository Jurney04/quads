Sightseeing

210€ per buggy

max 2 persons per buggy
combination with quads is possible

DESCRIPTION

This ride offers an adrenaline-filled off-road adventure of 3.30 hours. We start with a half-hour introduction to fully familiarize you with driving a buggy. Then we head into the mountains and along our route we discover six unique locations, with breathtaking views. Along the way we take two breaks where you can enjoy a cold drink and we also stop to eat a sandwich.

BESCHRIJVING

We first gather at the appointed place, then you get your helmet and bandana. Once everyone is ready and has received explanations, we set off on an adventure on dirt tracks and beautiful views. After a long ride, we will take a break on a mountain, where your have a fantastic view. After we have a drink, we continue into the mountains where there are challenging slopes and drops. Then we continue back on dirt roads among nature.

Are you interested in riding around on a quad bike? Then contact us to arrange a time.
