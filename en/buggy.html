Ride a buggy

Are you ready for an exhilarating adventure on four wheels?

At Quadadventures Murcia, we offer guided buggy rides, perfect for
anyone who wants to experience the thrill of riding a buggy.

110€ per buggy

Description - Intro Tour

One of our popular rides! In just one hour, feel the adrenaline of off-road driving as you cover 85% off-road trails. No stops, over rough terrain and curves that make your heart race. With dust rising and the sun on your face, Spain’s landscapes unfold – rolling hills, colorful fields, and breathtaking views. For young and old alike, this tour is the perfect intro to Quadadventures Murcia – short, powerful, and unforgettable!

Description - Lunch Tour

Our lunch tour takes you through authentic Spain. With 85% off-road, we ride hidden trails and cross stunning landscapes that rival the mountain route. Along the way, discover unique sights and soak up the true spirit of the Spanish countryside. The tour includes a free lunch in a real Spanish bar, where “la mama” herself prepares the meal. An unforgettable experience for all your senses!

Description - Mountain Tour

As the only company in the Murcia region, we take you up into the heights, where you experience pure fauna and flora up close. The ride to and from the mountain is an adventure in itself: the calm of the Spanish countryside, the sweeping views, and the challenging off-road tracks make this a journey you won’t forget. With 85% off-road, this tour is a true highlight for those who love thrill and nature. The dust? We gladly take it along, it’s all part of the adventure.

Sea Tour

160€ per buggy

Hover for more info

Description - Sea Tour

Our Sea Tour is no ordinary outing, but a carefully crafted premium excursion. The ride combines 35% off-road adventure with comfortable, dust-free stretches, giving you the best of both worlds. During the tour, we take an exclusive 45-minute break, where we provide refreshing chilled drinks. We also provide high-quality snorkeling gear, so you can explore the underwater world at ease. Everything is taken care of for you. Want to cool off? Be sure to bring your swimwear and a towel.

DESCRIPTION

We first gather at the appointed place, then you get your bandana. Once everyone is ready and has received explanations, we set off on an adventure on dirt tracks and beautiful views. After a long ride, we will take a break on a mountain, where your have a fantastic view. After we have a drink, we continue into the mountains where there are challenging slopes and drops. Then we continue back on dirt roads among nature.

Are you interested in riding around on a buggy? Then contact us to arrange a date and time.

DURING THE RIDE

Changing drivers during the journey is impossible

Use of electronic devices (mobile phone, camera, earphones)
is not allowed.

No alcohol/drugs.

No smoking during the entire ride and intermediate stops.

Prohibited throwing rubbish on the ground during stopovers.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Rental of up to 2 buggy’s and can be combined with max 4 quads

Children between 7-18 years ride along as a passenger (is also possible at the supervisor's quad for 25€)

Permitted weight per buggy: 150kg

Not suitable for pregnant women

Quadventures-Murcia is not responsible for the loss of personal items

Maximum of 2 people per buggy

During the ride, always keep your arms inside the buggy

We abide by the highway code

Comfortable clothing

Sunglasses

Good shoes (no flip-flops)
