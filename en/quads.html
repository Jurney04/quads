Are you ready for an exhilarating adventure on four wheels?

At Quad Adventures Murcia, we offer guided quad rides, perfect for
anyone who wants to experience the thrill of riding a quad bike.

DESCRIPTION

This ride offers an ideal opportunity to get acquainted with off-road adventure. Riding a quad or buggy is a dynamic experience that combines adrenaline and fun, navigating over a variety of terrain. This activity offers a unique, unforgettable experience for both beginners and experienced riders.

120€ per quad

DESCRIPTION

This ride offers an exciting off-road adventure of 2h30. We start with an introduction to get familiar with driving the quad bike. Then we explore the mountainous surroundings, enjoying breathtaking views. During the adventure we take two breaks, where you can relax with a refreshing cold drink.

3u30

Sightseeing

180€ per quad

*incl. fuel, insurance
combination with buggies is possible

DESCRIPTION

This ride offers an adrenaline-filled off-road adventure of 3.30 hours. We start with a half-hour introduction to fully familiarize you with driving the quad. Then we head into the mountains and along our route we discover six unique locations, with breathtaking views. Along the way we take two breaks, where you can enjoy a cold drink and we also stop to eat a sandwich.

DESCRIPTION

We first gather at the appointed place, then you get your helmet and bandana. Once everyone is ready and has received explanations, we set off on an adventure on dirt tracks and beautiful views. After a long ride, we will take a break on a mountain, where your have a fantastic view. After we have a drink, we continue into the mountains where there are challenging slopes and drops. Then we continue back on dirt roads among nature.

Are you interested in riding around on a quad bike? Then contact us to arrange a time.
